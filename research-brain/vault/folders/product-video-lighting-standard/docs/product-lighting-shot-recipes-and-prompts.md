# Product Lighting Shot Recipes and Prompt Templates

These recipes are intended to be adapted to the product. Do not copy them blindly; preserve the lighting logic and adjust background, material handling, and camera movement to the actual product.

## Shot Recipes

### Premium Package Hero Reveal

- **Mood:** premium neutral, controlled, precise.
- **Background:** deep charcoal or subtly graduated grey.
- **Key:** large soft key from camera-left at about 45 degrees and slightly above.
- **Fill:** white bounce one to two stops under.
- **Rim:** narrow rear-right strip source.
- **Reflection control:** black cards near front edges for crispness.
- **Shadow style:** soft contact shadow with defined grounding.
- **Camera:** slow arc or push-in, not both at once.

Prompt wording:

> photoreal premium package on dark neutral stage, single soft key from camera left, narrow rim light outlining edges, controlled reflections, soft grounded shadow, stable studio lighting throughout

Negative wording:

> random light shifts, blown highlights, neon spill, unstable reflections

### Kraft Paper Bag Commercial

- **Mood:** warm, tactile, natural premium.
- **Background:** warm stone, muted cream, or cafe-neutral brown-grey.
- **Key:** warm side light from camera-right or camera-left, broad and soft.
- **Fill:** very light top fill to preserve fold readability.
- **Rim:** optional soft back edge if the bag merges with the set.
- **Reflection control:** minimal; use negative fill to preserve contrast.
- **Shadow style:** soft but directional, showing base weight.
- **Camera:** slow vertical rise, diagonal push-in, or gentle three-quarter orbit.

Prompt wording:

> photoreal kraft paper bag, warm side light revealing paper texture, soft top fill, subtle natural falloff, tactile folds, grounded shadow, premium studio cafe aesthetic

Negative wording:

> dirty brown cast, flat front light, overexposed paper fibres, random colour shifts

### Flyer, Booklet, or Printing-Agency Ad

- **Mood:** clean, tactile, trustworthy.
- **Background:** white cyc, light grey tabletop, or subtle print-workshop desk.
- **Key:** large diffused top-front source to keep typography readable.
- **Accent:** restrained raking side light to reveal paper grain, embossing, folds, and page separation.
- **Fill:** bounce from opposite side.
- **Shadow style:** soft editorial shadow with no hard edge crossing text.
- **Camera:** top-down drift, page-turn macro, or shallow oblique slide.

Prompt wording:

> premium printed booklet on clean studio surface, broad soft overhead light for legibility, gentle grazing side light revealing paper texture and page depth, colour-accurate printing, refined editorial shadows

Negative wording:

> blown white paper, unreadable text blocks, harsh glare, muddy shadows

### Perfume Bottle Luxury Reveal

- **Mood:** luxurious, sculpted, glossy.
- **Background:** black acrylic, smoked grey gradient, or dense white for beauty e-commerce.
- **Key:** vertical softbox or large soft source offset from the bottle.
- **Rim:** one or two narrow strip lights for clean glass edges.
- **Fill:** tiny white cards or weak bounce, not broad frontal wash.
- **Reflection control:** black cards, strip masks, and cross-polarisation logic when needed.
- **Shadow style:** subtle; the bottle is often defined more by reflections than cast shadow.
- **Camera:** slow orbit with stable highlight travel.

Prompt wording:

> photoreal glass perfume bottle, clean vertical strip reflections, narrow rim highlights on the glass edges, controlled luxury studio lighting, visible liquid and refraction, stable reflections

Negative wording:

> milky glare, over-bloom, neon glow, random duplicated reflections, floating bottle

### Watch Macro Detail

- **Mood:** precision, engineering, micro-luxury.
- **Background:** black, stone, graphite, or restrained technical texture.
- **Key:** diffused top light close to the watch.
- **Fill:** white boards to recycle top-light information into the case.
- **Rim:** small softbox or narrow edge source for contour separation.
- **Accent:** one precise hard source for dial texture or indices.
- **Reflection control:** black flags very close to the watch when needed.
- **Camera:** locked macro push or micro-arc with extreme stability.

Prompt wording:

> photoreal luxury watch macro, diffused top light, crisp contour edge light, controlled micro-reflections on polished metal, readable dial detail, precision studio setup

Negative wording:

> noisy reflections, flat grey metal, soft unreadable dial, unstable highlight crawl

### Coffee Package or Cafe Product Ad

- **Mood:** warm, appetising, dimensional.
- **Background:** wood, stone, steam-softened cafe environment, or dark warm neutral.
- **Key:** side or slightly back-side directional light.
- **Fill:** white bounce low and opposite.
- **Accent:** tiny warm practicals or background bokeh if lifestyle-driven.
- **Shadow style:** soft feathered shadow with clear falloff.
- **Camera:** gentle lateral move or slow tabletop orbit.

Prompt wording:

> premium coffee product, warm directional side light, soft bounced fill, cafe atmosphere, dimensional tactile packaging, cinematic but realistic highlights, grounded tabletop shadow

Negative wording:

> flat overhead white light, cold sterile balance, muddy browns, clipped steam glow

### Clean Beauty or Cosmetics Advert

- **Mood:** bright, soft, clean, expensive.
- **Background:** white or pale neutral with controlled depth.
- **Key:** large frontal-above soft source.
- **Fill:** high but still slightly subordinate to the key.
- **Rim:** symmetrical restrained back edges if the container blends into the background.
- **Shadow style:** minimal but present; white-on-white still needs separation.
- **Camera:** frontal push, symmetric slide, or slow 180-degree turntable with static lighting.

Prompt wording:

> clean beauty product in bright white studio, broad soft key, smooth premium highlights, precise edge separation, colour-accurate packaging, minimal elegant shadows

Negative wording:

> washed-out edges, plastic glow, overexposed whites, cyan contamination

### Tech Product Reveal

- **Mood:** modern, precise, cool-neutral.
- **Background:** structured dark grey, black, or sophisticated white stage depending on brand.
- **Key:** broad side key.
- **Rim:** narrow linear strip or snooted edge highlight.
- **Fill:** low-intensity bounce to maintain industrial crispness.
- **Reflection control:** black flags prevent flare and keep logos readable.
- **Shadow style:** firmer than beauty lighting, but still clean.
- **Camera:** controlled arc or tilt detail reveal.

Prompt wording:

> photoreal modern tech product, cool-neutral studio lighting, clean linear edge reflections, controlled contrast, readable branding, precision commercial look

Negative wording:

> fake neon, rainbow reflections, logo flare, random lighting shifts

### Logo Animation Lighting Setup

- **Mood:** premium branded reveal.
- **Background:** match the brand system; usually dark neutral or clean white.
- **Key:** one dominant source establishing bevels and depth.
- **Fill:** low and broad.
- **Accent:** narrow travelling highlight or rim can work if the logo is metallic or glassy, but only one such animation should dominate.

Prompt wording:

> premium 3D logo reveal, single sculpting key with clean bevel highlights, restrained fill, elegant edge separation, stable studio lighting, brand-accurate colour

Negative wording:

> overdone lens flares, excessive bloom, disco colour shifts, unreadable silhouette

### Dark Luxury Product Stage

- **Mood:** sculptural, minimalist, high-contrast.
- **Background:** black or nearly black, with controlled gradation only if needed.
- **Key:** lateral softbox slightly behind the subject.
- **Rim:** narrow snooted or strip source for separation.
- **Fill:** almost none except micro-bounces on critical brand details.
- **Reflection control:** black cards define crispness.
- **Shadow style:** deep and sparse, but not muddy.

Prompt wording:

> luxury product floating in darkness, sculptural side key, narrow controlled edge highlight, minimal fill, black stage, precise speculars, high-end still-life aesthetic

Negative wording:

> muddy blacks, broad wash, glowing halos, unreadable product face

### Bright Minimal White Studio Setup

- **Mood:** clean, airy, commercial, catalogue-plus.
- **Background:** white acrylic or white cyc.
- **Background light:** balanced near paper white rather than wildly clipped.
- **Object light:** separate key to preserve edges.
- **Camera:** frontal or slight three-quarter move.

Prompt wording:

> premium white studio product shot, clean high-key lighting, preserved product edges against white background, subtle grounding shadow, soft commercial reflections

Negative wording:

> edge loss, stray flare, flat front light, clipping on product highlights

## Base Prompt Templates

### Premium Product Lighting

> Photoreal [product] in a controlled studio environment. Single soft key light from camera left, slightly above the product. Gentle fill from camera right, two stops lower. Narrow rim light outlining the product edges from rear right. Clean reflections matched to the material. Soft contact shadow grounding the product. Stable studio lighting throughout the shot. Brand-accurate colour. Premium commercial look.

### White-Background Product Work

> Photoreal [product] on a clean white studio background. Bright high-key lighting with preserved edge separation. Large soft overhead-front key, subtle side fill, gentle grounding shadow, no overexposure on the product surface, colour-accurate whites, premium catalogue-commercial quality.

### Dark Luxury Product Work

> Photoreal [product] on a black studio stage. Sculpted low-key lighting with a soft lateral key, precise edge highlight, minimal fill, controlled specular reflections, clean silhouette separation, refined high-contrast commercial aesthetic.

### Glass or Perfume

> Photoreal glass [product] with clean vertical strip reflections, controlled edge highlights, visible liquid and refraction, soft grounded shadow, no milky glare, no artificial glow, stable reflections throughout the shot.

### Paper and Print

> Photoreal printed [booklet/flyer/book], broad diffused light for legibility, gentle grazing side light revealing paper texture and page depth, colour-accurate print, no blown whites, refined editorial-commercial lighting.

### Recommended Veo-Style Negative Prompt

> random light shifts, exposure flicker, unstable reflections, neon spill, plastic-looking highlights, muddy shadows, clipped blacks, blown paper whites, excessive bloom, artificial haloing
