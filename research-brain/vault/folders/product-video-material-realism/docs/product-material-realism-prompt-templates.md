# Product Material Realism Prompt Templates

These templates are designed to be copied and adapted to a specific product, lighting plan, and shot length. They assume a product-video workflow where material realism matters.

## Master Template

```md
[Cinematography]
Macro close-up / medium product shot / slow dolly in / controlled arc shot.

[Subject]
The exact product from the reference image, preserving proportions, label layout, materials, edges, and brand colours.

[Material behaviour]
Describe the visible surface precisely:
base colour, roughness, metallic or dielectric behaviour, clear coat, fibre texture, micro-scratches, paper thickness, embossed print, glass refraction, liquid depth, brushed grain, leather pores, matte or glossy finish, slightly rounded bevels, subtle edge wear.

[Action]
Only the visible motion:
gentle turntable rotation / slight cap movement / slow reflection travel / subtle steam drift / paper page turn / soft bag deformation.

[Lighting]
Controlled studio lighting, softbox or strip reflections placed intentionally, realistic rim or side light revealing texture, stable shadows, clean contact shadow grounding the product, no random light shifts.

[Context]
Minimal premium studio environment, clean background, no distracting props unless explicitly requested.

[Style and finish]
Real studio-shot commercial footage, physically plausible materials, restrained post-processing, premium brand-ad aesthetic.

[Negative constraints]
label distortion, warped geometry, floating object, plastic-looking sheen, random glow, excessive bloom, fake sparkles, inconsistent reflections, oversmoothed texture, muddy shadows, duplicated details, text artefacts.
```

## Negative Prompt Template

```md
Negative constraints:
plastic-looking surface, waxy finish, random glow, excessive bloom, fake sparkle effects, inconsistent material texture, warped label text, bent geometry, floating object, reflection mismatch, muddy or doubled shadows, over-sharpened edges, oversaturated colours, repeated details, unstable highlights, noisy chrome, low-detail surface.
```

## Perfume Bottle Material Realism

```md
Macro commercial beauty shot of the exact perfume bottle from the reference image. Slow dolly in with a subtle left-to-right reflection drift across the glass shoulder. Thick clear glass with sharp edge refraction, clean vertical softbox reflections, faint internal liquid meniscus, polished metal cap with controlled highlight, embossed logo catching a gentle rim light. Dark luxury studio background, stable lighting, premium cosmetics advert, real studio-shot feel. Negative constraints: magical sparkles, cloudy glass, melted reflections, warped bottle proportions, floating bottle, random bloom, label distortion.
```

## Kraft Paper Bag Material Realism

```md
Medium close product shot of the exact kraft paper bag from the reference image. Slow controlled arc shot. Natural uncoated kraft paper, warm beige-brown base colour, visible long paper fibres, low-sheen matte surface, slightly compressed folded seams, subtle edge darkening, realistic print ink absorption, soft side light revealing texture, gentle top fill, soft contact shadow on tabletop. Clean premium packaging advert, tactile and realistic. Negative constraints: glossy paper, plastic sheen, overly smooth surface, warped handles, fake folds, random glow, floating bag.
```

## Flyer and Booklet Printing Advert Material Realism

```md
Macro-to-medium product reveal of the printed flyer and booklet set from the reference image. Slow top-down push-in with slight rack focus from the paper edge to the printed cover. Thick premium stock, crisp die-cut edges, visible paper thickness, subtle uncoated grain, slight emboss on the title, ink sitting naturally on paper, soft grazing side light revealing the surface, clean white tabletop, realistic studio commercial. Negative constraints: paper looking like plastic, blurry print, bent geometry, overexposed whites, mirrored reflections, floating pages, repeated print patterns.
```

## Watch and Metal Macro Detail

```md
Extreme macro shot of the exact watch from the reference image. Slow orbit around the dial at product level. Brushed stainless steel case with directional anisotropic grain, polished bevel catching a thin sharp strip highlight, sapphire crystal with restrained rectangular reflection, matte dial texture, precise engraved crown detail, shallow depth of field with the logo and indices remaining readable. Real luxury watch advertisement, stable studio lighting, physically plausible reflections. Negative constraints: noisy chrome, liquid-looking metal, warped indices, unreadable dial text, random lens flare, excessive bloom.
```

## Cosmetics Glass and Liquid Realism

```md
Cinematic close-up of the exact skincare bottle and liquid texture from the reference image. Gentle camera push-in. Frosted glass body, smooth diffused transmission, clean edge highlights, visible liquid depth inside, soft cap reflection, subtle condensation droplets only if visible on the source image, bright minimal beauty studio lighting with controlled white reflections. High-end clean beauty advert. Negative constraints: fake glitter, over-glossy plastic cap, cloudy product, colour drift, unstable reflections, warped nozzle or cap geometry.
```

## Coffee Packaging Material Realism

```md
Premium cafe product shot of the exact coffee package from the reference image. Slow dolly in with slight parallax. Matte printed coffee bag with visible fold memory, subtle creases near the seal, realistic print finish, one-way valve detail, soft warm side light, gentle practical ambience, controlled highlight on edges, grounded contact shadow. Real boutique coffee commercial, tactile packaging, not synthetic. Negative constraints: glossy plastic look, floating bag, overdone steam, warped typography, random shine, muddy dark areas.
```

## ARTIZ.AZ Production Workflow Template

```md
Use the supplied reference image as the source of truth for the exact product design. Preserve proportions, label placement, colours, materials, and surface details exactly. Prompt for motion only: a slow premium commercial camera move with stable studio lighting, controlled reflections, realistic contact shadows, and physically plausible material behaviour. Emphasise tactile surface qualities such as paper fibre, embossed print, brushed metal, clear glass refraction, leather pores, or matte polymer micro-texture as appropriate to the product. The result should look like high-end studio-shot commercial footage created by a major production company. Negative constraints: redesign of the product, inconsistent materials across frames, random light shifts, warped geometry, floating objects, plastic-looking sheen, fake glow, repeated details, label distortion, AI-looking texture smoothing.
```
