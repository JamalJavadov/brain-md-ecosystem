# Ring for Baku AI Jewelry Prompt Templates

The most important prompt principle is to prompt for the scene, person, and camera behavior, not for final jewelry geometry. When the exact product matters, the product itself should come from a real reference image or locked reference asset.

Prompting should control the human context around the product: age feel, styling direction, mood, pose, lens character, surface realism, lighting logic, and background atmosphere.

## General Formula

```text
[model type]
+ [pose]
+ [wardrobe]
+ [environment]
+ [camera/lens look]
+ [lighting logic]
+ [skin and texture realism]
+ [brand mood]
+ [constraint that product remains unchanged]
```

The model type controls who appears and whether they fit the audience. The pose controls where the jewelry sits and whether it reads clearly. Wardrobe determines whether the product feels cheap, costume-like, street, or luxury. Lens look controls perspective distortion and emotional distance. Lighting logic makes metal believable. Texture realism prevents waxy skin and plastic surfaces. The final constraint reminds the model not to remake the product.

When the image model supports reference images, keep this instruction constant across campaigns:

```text
the jewelry remains unchanged from the product reference
```

This can reduce product drift, but exactness still requires reference-led compositing.

## Gothic Lifestyle Portrait

```text
A photorealistic editorial portrait of a young adult model in dark minimalist clothing, standing in a moody urban night setting, subtle gothic styling, clean natural skin texture, realistic pores and hair detail, calm self-assured expression, cinematic low-key lighting with controlled metal highlights, shallow depth of field, premium fashion photography look, the jewelry remains exactly as in the reference product, no redesign, no extra ornaments, no fantasy elements.
```

Use this when mood comes first and product detail comes second. It is suitable for brand-building posts.

Realism levers:

- Natural skin texture.
- Controlled metal highlights.
- No redesign.

Negative prompt ideas:

```text
extra fingers, fused jewelry, plastic skin, over-smoothed face, melted metal, fake tattoos, noisy shadows, fantasy armor, duplicate accessories, wrong jewelry scale
```

## Viking-Inspired Ring Close-Up

```text
Extreme close-up of a real stainless steel statement ring worn naturally on a human hand, masculine rugged styling, realistic finger anatomy, visible knuckles, natural nail texture, sharp metal edges, accurate engraved detail, cool directional light with soft fill, premium macro jewelry photography, dark stone or leather background, photorealistic reflections, authentic skin contact, no distortion.
```

Use this as a conversion-oriented prompt for ring detail.

Realism levers:

- Realistic finger anatomy.
- Accurate engraved detail.
- Authentic skin contact.

Negative prompt ideas:

```text
deformed finger joints, extra fingernails, warped band shape, floating ring, fake engraving, low-detail metal, blurry edges, cartoon reflections
```

## Biker Streetwear Model Image

```text
Photorealistic streetwear portrait, black leather or heavy cotton layers, urban industrial environment, confident pose, ring and pendant styled as premium stainless steel accessories, realistic body posture, subtle skin imperfections, controlled flash and ambient mix, gritty but polished editorial look, no exaggerated cyberpunk effects, no costume feel, product stays true to reference.
```

Use this for younger male and unisex audiences.

The phrase "gritty but polished" matters because biker aesthetics can easily become costume-like.

Negative prompt ideas:

```text
over-dramatic smoke, neon overload, fake chrome skin, impossible shadows, duplicated chains, fantasy biker gear
```

## Dark Luxury Necklace Image

```text
Elegant close-up portrait focused on neck and upper chest, premium black wardrobe, clean neckline, pendant sitting naturally with gravity, chain following collarbone and fabric contours, soft directional studio lighting, refined specular highlights on stainless steel, luxury campaign aesthetic, authentic skin detail, restrained composition, photorealistic fashion image.
```

Use this for necklaces and pendants. The critical phrase is "pendant sitting naturally with gravity" because floating necklace mistakes quickly break realism.

Negative prompt ideas:

```text
floating chain, asymmetrical chain length, over-retouched skin, unnatural clavicle, plastic shine, broken links, duplicated pendant
```

