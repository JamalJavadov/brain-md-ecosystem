# MagicStory Instagram Brand Content Standard

## When to use this file

Use this as the baseline before creating or reviewing any MagicStory Instagram feed post, carousel visual, reference-style adaptation, or image-generation prompt. It captures the durable brand rules from the MagicStory content workflow.

## Business Positioning

MagicStory is a creative gift brand that prepares personalized magical storybooks for children.

Core product idea:

- The child becomes the hero of the story.
- The book can include the child's name, photo or visual character, appearance, interests, family or loved ones, personal world, favorite themes, and adventures.
- The final product is not only a book; it is an emotional keepsake gift.

Position MagicStory as:

- a personalized children's book;
- a magical gift experience;
- an emotional memory for parents;
- a joyful reading experience for children;
- a premium and sentimental birthday or special-occasion gift.

Primary brand line:

```text
Hər uşağın öz sehrli hekayəsi var.
```

Instagram handle:

```text
magicstory.az
```

## Content Promise

MagicStory posts should sell the feeling that the child sees themselves inside a magical world.

Do not frame posts as generic book ads. The stronger promise is:

```text
Uşaq sadəcə kitab oxumur — öz nağılının qəhrəmanı olur.
```

Other useful emotional statements:

```text
Hər uşağın içində bir qəhrəman yaşayır.
```

```text
Hər uşağın öz sehrli hekayəsi var.
```

## Visual Identity

Use a warm, magical, premium, child-friendly, emotional, and imaginative visual world.

Confirmed palette:

- warm golden yellow matching the MagicStory logo;
- cream;
- beige;
- kraft brown;
- caramel or golden brown;
- soft blue or navy when needed for night or magic scenes;
- red backgrounds when matching established mascot reference imagery.

When adapting a reference image, replace unsuitable reference colors with MagicStory yellow/gold when appropriate.

## Mascot Rules

MagicStory has a recurring custom mascot:

- cute childlike magical boy;
- fluffy brown hair;
- big expressive eyes;
- warm friendly expression;
- cream and golden-yellow outfit;
- small cape;
- book, star, or magic details on clothing;
- suitable for children's book branding.

The mascot may be stylized into different formats, including:

- 2D cartoon;
- plush or felt look;
- fuzzy 3D close-up;
- pixel art;
- fantasy hero;
- paper-cut illustration;
- painterly cartoon.

The mascot must remain recognizable across posts. Keep the hair, face, outfit, cape, and cream/gold palette consistent whenever possible.

Do not turn the mascot into a direct copy of a reference character. Use references only for style, composition, mood, layout, or energy.

## Packaging Rules

The MagicStory box is a major premium trust signal. It must match the real packaging and must not look pasted into a generated scene.

Required box traits:

- flat kraft cardboard mailer or gift-box shape;
- correct cardboard folds and mailer proportions;
- cream/gold MagicStory vertical label or wrap across the front/lid area;
- gold logo;
- magical moon, stars, and open-box symbol;
- subtle premium branding;
- handmade gift feeling.

Possible text on packaging:

```text
MagicStory
Hər uşaq öz nağılının qəhrəmanıdır.
Sevgi ilə hazırlanıb
```

Integration requirements:

- match the scene lighting;
- match perspective;
- match scale;
- use grounded shadows;
- preserve kraft cardboard material texture;
- if held by a character, make the hand grip, arm pose, gaze, and body orientation support the action naturally.

Avoid:

- generic parcel designs;
- wrong proportions;
- invented labels or extra unrelated box details;
- pasted or floating look;
- box lighting that does not match the scene;
- incorrect perspective or unsupported hand grip.

## Book Rules

When MagicStory books are shown, they should read as personalized children's books, not generic documents.

For the folder/document-style post, the confirmed book constraint was:

```text
Bütün kitablar eyni ölçüdə olmalıdır: 28x21 sm horizontal format.
```

English working version:

```text
All books should be the same size: 28x21 cm horizontal format.
```

## Reference Adaptation Rules

The common user instruction was:

```text
tamam indi isə bunu indi göndərdiyim style da bizim biznesə uyğun hazırla
```

Interpretation: create a MagicStory version in the same style as the uploaded reference, adapted to the business.

For every reference-based post:

- match the reference's style, mood, layout, and composition language;
- do not copy the exact original content;
- adapt the concept to MagicStory's personalized children's book business;
- use logo, mascot, gift box, books, moon, stars, open-book motifs, or open-box motifs when relevant;
- use Azerbaijani text when text appears;
- keep visuals premium, magical, child-friendly, and Instagram-ready.

Default aspect ratio:

```text
3:4
```

Use 3:4 for MagicStory Instagram feed posts unless the user specifies otherwise.

- use Azerbaijani;
- keep it short and readable;

- expect possible spelling errors from image-generation models;

## Copyright And Style Safety

Many user references may resemble recognizable third-party properties. Do not directly recreate copyrighted characters, exact scenes, logos, names, or signature protected designs.

Use references for:

- composition;
- mood;
- color;
- energy;
- layout;
- style language.

For recognizable references such as Disney, Tangled, Adventure Time, Spider-Man, Minions, or Shaun the Sheep, make an original MagicStory-themed reinterpretation instead of a direct remake.

## Product Flow To Communicate

The content can explain the customer and product flow:

1. Parent or customer contacts MagicStory.
2. MagicStory collects information about the child.
3. Information may include name, photo or visual identity, age, interests, favorite colors, animals, heroes, family, loved ones, and desired theme.
4. MagicStory creates a personalized story.
5. The child becomes the hero.
6. The book is printed or prepared.
7. The book is packaged in the MagicStory gift box.
8. The product is given as a magical emotional gift.
9. The parent and child receive a keepsake memory.

## Recurring Post Categories

Useful recurring content categories:

- emotional quote;
- behind-the-scenes process;
- product or box showcase;
- mascot adventure;
- birthday gift;
- customer education;
- DM/order explanation.

Birthday and gift framing matter because many orders are birthday-related. Customers may DM first and order later, so posts should build trust, stay memorable, and sometimes remind parents to order ahead.

## Review Checklist

Before publishing or accepting an output, check:

- Does it clearly communicate personalized children's books, not just generic magic?
- Does the child-as-hero idea come through?
- Is the MagicStory yellow/gold palette present where appropriate?
- Is the mascot recognizable if used?
- Does the box match the real kraft mailer packaging?
- Does the box look naturally integrated rather than pasted?
- Is Azerbaijani text spelled correctly?
- Is the visual sufficiently original if based on a recognizable reference?
- Is the post 3:4 unless another format was requested?
- Is the CTA simple and DM-friendly when copy is included?
