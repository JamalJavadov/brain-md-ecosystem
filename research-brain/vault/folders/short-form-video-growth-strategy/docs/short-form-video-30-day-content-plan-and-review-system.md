# Short-Form Video 30-Day Content Plan and Review System

This 30-day plan mixes searchable utility, transformation, shareability, creator voice, UGC-style proof, and deliberate testing. It is useful when a creator or business needs a month of structured output instead of random daily posting.

## Operating Rules

Before posting, check that:

- The first frame is understandable without context.
- The hook states a payoff, problem, or tension.
- The product, person, or point is visible on a small screen.
- Captions are readable and inside safe zones.
- Visual changes occur whenever attention would otherwise stall.
- The ending resolves the promise made at the start.
- The title or caption uses clear topic words.
- The audio is legally usable for the account type and purpose.

## Weekly Review Buckets

At the end of each week, sort posts into four buckets:

| Bucket | What it means | Next action |
|---|---|---|
| Strong opener / strong finish | The hook and payoff both worked | Make three more on the same topic cluster |
| Strong opener / weak finish | The concept stopped the scroll but did not sustain attention | Tighten the middle and improve the payoff |
| Weak opener / strong finish | The video was good after people entered, but too few entered | Rewrite hooks and first frames |
| Weak opener / weak finish | The idea, packaging, or execution failed | Change the topic angle before editing variants |

Use Instagram Trial Reels, YouTube viewed-versus-swiped-away and retention, and TikTok search and post analytics to support this review.

## Improvement Loop

The strongest creators repeatedly do four things:

1. Choose a topic people already care about.
2. Package it clearly in the first second.
3. Keep attention moving with purposeful editing.
4. Learn from the metrics without changing variables randomly.

This is the durable system behind more views, higher retention, and more viewers watching to the end.
